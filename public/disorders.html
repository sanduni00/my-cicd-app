Common Disorders

  • Depression: Persistent sadness, lack of energy, and loss of interest in life.
  • Anxiety: Excessive fear, nervousness, and worry that disrupts daily life.
  • Bipolar Type1: Alternating periods of depression and elevated mood (mania).
  • PTSD: Anxiety and flashbacks after traumatic events.
  • OCD: Intrusive thoughts and repetitive behaviors.
